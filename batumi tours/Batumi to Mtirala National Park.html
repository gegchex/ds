Adventure tour from Batumi to Mtirala National Park

General Tour Description:

This scenic day hike in Mtirala National Park offers a perfect blend of natural beauty and adventure, available from May to October. The easy, circular trail covers 7 kilometers, starting from the visitorsâ€™ center and winding along the picturesque Chakvistskali River. The path leads towards the stunning Tsablnari Waterfall, which stands over 15 meters tall. Along the way, you'll pass vibrant clusters of blooming rhododendrons, a truly breathtaking sight in spring. For thrill-seekers, the route features a zip-line, allowing you to soar between the trees or you can enjoy Rope Park experience with two trails: an adult trail featuring 6 elements over 67 meters, and a family trail with 11 elements stretching 142 meters; Enjoy a delightful lunch at a charming Mtirala Park Resort, where you can savor delicious regional flavors in relaxed and inviting green surrounding.

  • Visit Martvili Monastery (on request);

    Situated on a hilltop, Martvili Monastery offers stunning views of the Kolkheti Valley and the Caucasus mountains. Originally built in the 7th century on a pre-Christian site, the monastery showcases fascinating history and architecture. Nestled in serene surroundings, this spot offers the perfect escape for those seeking breathtaking scenery and peaceful ambiance.

  • Visit Prometheus Cave;

    Prometheus Cave is a captivating underground world of stalagmites and stalactites, sparking the imagination with its otherworldly beauty. The softly illuminated chambers create a magical atmosphere as you stroll along the well-designed 1,420-meter path, passing through five magnificent underground halls. For an added touch of adventure, visitors can also enjoy an optional boat ride, offering a unique view of enchanting underground landscape. Please, be aware: Visit to Prometheus cave and boat trip could be restricted due to the weather conditions.
