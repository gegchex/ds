Family trip Magnetic beach – Ureki

General Tour Description:
This scenic day hike in Mtirala National Park offers a perfect blend of natural beauty and adventure, available from May to October. The easy, circular trail covers 7 kilometers, starting from the visitors’ center and winding along the picturesque Chakvistskali River. The path leads towards the stunning Tsablnari Waterfall, which stands over 15 meters tall. Along the way, you'll pass vibrant clusters of blooming rhododendrons, a truly breathtaking sight in spring. For thrill-seekers, the route features a zip-line, allowing you to soar between the trees or you can enjoy Rope Park experience with two trails: an adult trail featuring 6 elements over 67 meters, and a family trail with 11 elements stretching 142 meters; Enjoy a delightful lunch at a charming Mtirala Park Resort, where you can savor delicious regional flavors in relaxed and inviting green surrounding.
  • Departure from Batumi;
  • Visit Mtirala National Park Visitor’s center;
  • Start Hiking Route “Tsablnari”;

    The circular route starts from the visitors’ center and goes upstream of the Chakvistskali river. On the road there will be a mechanical mini-cable car, with the help of which you can get to the other side of the river and continue the path to the waterfall. On the way, rhododendron grows in large numbers, the flowering of which is a very beautiful sight. After 1.5 km, you will come to a zip-line, specially installed here for thrill-seekers. Here you can ride on ropes between the trees. A few meters from the zip-line, the road forks. If you want to see only the beautiful river backwater, which is called here “the lake”, then you need to turn left, if you are heading to the waterfall, then you should turn right. After passing 1.5 km, you will come to the Tsablnari waterfall (more than 15 m high). Back from the waterfall, after walking 1 km, if you wish, you will get to the river backwater. On the route your can try: ZIP line and Rope Park.

  • Hiking: Route – “Tsablnari”
  • Available: May-October;
  • Total length: 7 km;
  • Altitude 260 – 445 m above sea level;
  • Difficulty level – easy.
  • ZIP line:
  • Total length – 220 m.
  • Rope Park.

    1. Trail for adults: it contains 6 elements. The adult’s height must not be less than 1 meter and 20 cm in order to use the service. The total length of the trail is 67 meters.
    2. Trail for family trip: it contains 11 elements. The adult’s height must not be less than 1 meter and 20 cm in order to us the service. The total length of the trail is 142 meters.

  • Lunch at Park Resort Mtirala(on request);

    Discover Mtirala Park Resort, where relaxation, entertainment, and culinary delights come together in a single, vibrant space. For those seeking thrills, the Maverick tour and Velo zipline promise exhilarating adventures, while "sport" shooting offers a fun challenge. Unwind at our hotel, complete with a refreshing pool, and enjoy a variety of delicious dining options.

  • Return to Batumi.
